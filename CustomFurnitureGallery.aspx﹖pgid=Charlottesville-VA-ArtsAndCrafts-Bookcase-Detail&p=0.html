DETAIL ARTS AND CRAFTS BOOKCASE 15558.2
Charlottesville, VA

Mortise and tenon joinery is one of the keystones in the tradition of fine woodworking, but this overlapping tenon secures this bookcase’s style, as confidently as it secures the form’s structure. A detail clearly associated with the work of the Greene brothers, the joinery sets the piece firmly in the "Bungalow Style," of the "Arts and Crafts" movement.
