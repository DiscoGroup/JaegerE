NANTUCKET HERITAGE KITCHEN A 20138.1
Nantucket, MA

Hand-picked floorboards, a beaded ceiling with exposed beams, and a gas-range hearth, crafted from recycled brick, offer the inviting comfort of a well-worn workspace. The painted, custom cabinets maintain the line of the room’s painted door casings, below the beam bottoms, to preserve a sense of extra space, above.
